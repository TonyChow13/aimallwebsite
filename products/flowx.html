03 Feature spotlight

Keep goods flowing as planned

FlowX follows products, cartons and orders from the distribution center to the last mile. It checks counts on the packing line, catches mis-sorts, and matches every manifest against what actually went out the door.

  • Line-side count & SKU verification
  • Manifest accuracy and mis-sort detection
  • Live status from backroom to dispatch

04 Feature spotlight

Eyes on every driver and vehicle

FlowX continues out onto the road. In-cab monitoring picks up fatigue, smoking or phone use, and the fleet view scores each vehicle and route as it goes, so problems show up before they turn into incidents.

  • In-cab driver monitoring (fatigue, smoking, phone)
  • Live vehicle scoring, routing & geofencing
  • Exception alerts across the whole fleet

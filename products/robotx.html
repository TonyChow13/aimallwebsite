03 Feature spotlight

One brain for a mixed fleet

RobotX sits above your robots, edge devices and staff and runs the whole mix. When a task comes in from the A³V loop, RobotX assigns it to the closest capable resource: a robot, an edge device, or a person with a phone.

  • Heterogeneous dispatch across robots, devices & people
  • Nearest-capable-resource task assignment
  • Driven directly by the A³V execution loop

04 Feature spotlight

Validated in simulation before it runs

Before a robot moves, RobotX rehearses the plan in a Sim2Real environment and checks the route against the real floor for safety and feasibility. Once the work is done, RobotX confirms completion back to the system. Nothing counts as finished until that check passes.

  • Sim2Real rehearsal before real-world execution
  • Safety & feasibility checks against the live floor
  • Closed-loop verification of every completed task

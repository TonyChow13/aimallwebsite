03 Feature spotlight

Shelf audit, down to the SKU

Take one photo of the shelf and SnapX gives you the whole picture: every SKU, how many facings it has, and whether it's where the planogram says it should be. It also reads on-shelf availability, share of shelf and golden-zone ratio, so the field team can fix gaps before they cost a sale.

  • On-shelf availability, share-of-shelf & facings-per-SKU
  • Golden-zone ratio and display-rule supervision
  • Out-of-stock prediction and competitor tracking

04 Feature spotlight

The only frozen-display recognition in China

Glass doors, reflections and packed freezers throw off most vision systems. SnapX handles them anyway, picking out freezer items, price tags, POSM and assets. That's what lets it run live market audits across more than 20 supermarket chains.

  • Display segmentation through glass-door glare
  • Price-tag, promotion and POSM recognition
  • Asset tracking and inventory recognition
